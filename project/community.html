Connecting with our Community

Christ Church has been involved in Neighbors in Need, Teen Feed, University Churches Emergency Fund and the founding of the Emergency Feeding Program.

We have participated in the annual AIDS Walk and been part of AIDS Care teams. Deacon Mary Shehane, who has been the chaplain to Canterbury (now Episcopal Crossroads) founded the Sanctuary Art Center while at Christ Church.

Currently, Christ Church houses a SHARE (Seattle Housing & Resource Eort) indoor shelter for 30 men on a nightly basis, and provides a monthly meal at the Orion Center downtown.

“We emerge with abundant hope, better discipline for working together and with partners beyond this Church, for our fundamental reason for being – engagement with God’s mission.” - Presiding Bishop, Katharine Jeerts Schori
