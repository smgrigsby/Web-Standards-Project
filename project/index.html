Announcements

Welcome home Pr. Rachel Endicott who will celebrate and preach at the 9:30 AM Rite I Holy Eucharist service in the church. Morning Prayer will be read at the 1928 Prayerbook service in the Chapel at 10 AM.

During August, the 9:30 AM service will be a Rite I Holy Eucharist.

Children's Choir: The third summer children's choir rehearsal is Sunday, August 5 at 8:30 AM. We'll be in the undercroft unless the piano gets moved by then.

Nursery & Church School: The Nursery is not staffed July & August. However, families may use the space to do-it-yourself (DIY) during the 9:30 service.

For more information, download the pdf copy of this week’s GOOD NEWS BRIEF.

Summer Schedule July 1-September 2

  • 9:30 AM Rite II Holy Eucharist in the Church
  • 10:00 AM 1928 Prayerbook Service, Chapel
  • 11:00 AM Adult Education, Wilson Hall
  • Coffee Hour, Undercroft

Other meetings will be adjusted accordingly.

Parking Options

  1. In the lot next to the church (Sunday only, free until noon)
  2. In the lot at Carson’s Cleaners (free in the evening and on Sunday)
  3. Street Parking (free after 6pm and on Sunday)
  4. During other times, parking tokens available in the church office
